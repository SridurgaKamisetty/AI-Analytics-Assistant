# Enterprise AI Analytics Assistant

## Vision

Build an AI-powered analytics assistant that enables business users to analyze company data using natural language.

## Business Problem

Managers spend too much time switching between Excel, SQL, and Power BI to answer business questions.

## Solution

Provide a single AI assistant that can:
- Read business data
- Calculate KPIs
- Generate summaries
- Recommend actions
- Answer questions in natural language

## Target Users

- CEO
- Sales Manager
- HR Manager
- Finance Manager
- Business Analyst
